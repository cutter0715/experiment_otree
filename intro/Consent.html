This is a part of academic research project under the acceptance by research ethics committee of Graduate School of Business Administration, Kobe University, Japan. You are invited to participate in a research study. This form includes information about the study and contact information if you have any questions. The purpose of this research is to study management control.

What will my participation involve?

If you decide to participate in this research, you will complete a computer-based task and a survey. This study will take place online using Prolific (https://www.prolific.co). You will first read a set of instructions to learn how the task page will look like. There will be short quizzes to help you understand the set up. Next, you will participate in the experiment, followed by a survey. Lastly, you will collect the earnings from the experiment. The payment will not be earned unless you conduct all tasks and answer survey followed by the tasks.

Will I be compensated for my participation?

At the end of the experiment, you will receive a code that you have to enter on Prolific website to receive your payment. Following the completion of all sessions of this study you cash payment per point will be determined. The participants will receive something between about $10 and $20, depending on the number of points earned, to result in an expected average compensation around $15.

Are there any risks to me?

Your participation in the study is completely voluntary. You may withdraw your consent to participate in this study at any time during if you so desire by exiting the task. Every effort will be made to ensure your confidentiality. The information that you provide and the decisions that you make during the experiment will be recorded; however, only your Subject code/ID generated by Prolific website will identify you. Your responses are anonymous, so no one can associate your answers back to you. Please do not include your name or other identifying information in your responses that can identify you. The experiment involves no interaction amongst the participants, no other participant can see the decisions made by you.

Who can I contact with questions?

If you have any questions or concerns about the research, please feel free to contact the investigator, Ryota Kataoka, a Ph.D. student at 200b003b(atmark)stu.kobe-u.ac.jp.
